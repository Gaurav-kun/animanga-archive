────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  WHAT IT IS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

A personal, hand-curated archive tracking every anime, manga,
and manhwa I watched or read in 2025–26. No algorithms. No
filler. Just my genuine picks — rated, tagged, and linkable.

Features:
  •  Daily picks (random anime/manga/manhwa of the day)
  •  Favorites system with export-to-text
  •  Real-time search & multi-genre filtering
  •  Sort by rating, year, title
  •  Grid / list view toggle
  •  Keyboard shortcuts (1/2/3 → jump sections, F → favs, D → dojo)
  •  Sound effects (Web Audio API — sword clicks & chimes)
  •  LocalStorage persistence (entries, favorites, progress)
  •  "Add Entry" form (saves forever to localStorage)
